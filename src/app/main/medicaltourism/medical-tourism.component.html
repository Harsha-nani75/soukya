Frequently Asked Questions

Questions, you should have a many, while making a decision for advanced treatment. We have listed a few questions people generally ask, for your knowledge. If you have any questions other than these, please write to us at support@soukhya.health.

  • What is medical tourism?

    Medical tourism refers to traveling to another country to receive medical treatment. In the case of India, it often involves seeking medical care at renowned hospitals or clinics for procedures ranging from cardiac surgeries to cosmetic surgeries.

  • Why should I consider India for medical treatment?

    India offers world-class medical facilities, highly skilled doctors, and cost-effective treatments, making it a compelling choice for medical treatment.

  • What types of medical treatments are available in India?

    India provides a wide range of medical treatments, including cardiac surgery, orthopaedic procedures, organ transplants, cancer treatment, cosmetic surgery, fertility treatments, and more. The country is known for its expertise in complex surgeries and advanced medical procedures.

  • What language barriers might I encounter during my medical visit to India?

    English is widely spoken and understood in India, especially in urban areas and at major hospitals catering to international patients. Many medical professionals in India are fluent in English, which helps facilitate communication between patients and healthcare providers.
