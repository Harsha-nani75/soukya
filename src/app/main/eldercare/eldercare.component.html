Frequently Asked Questions

Questions, you should have many, while making a decision for advanced treatment. We have listed a few questions people generally ask about your knowledge. If you have any further questions, please feel free to write to us at support@soukhya.health

  • What is elder care, and who can benefit from it?

    Elder care focuses on addressing the unique healthcare needs of older adults, including medical, social, and emotional aspects. Anyone aged 60 and older or experiencing age-related health issues can benefit from elder care.

  • What types of services are included in elder care, and how can they help me or my loved one?

    Elder care services may include medical assessment, medication management, regular health check-ups, physical therapy, pain management, mental health support, and end-of-life care.

  • How can I access elder care services in India, especially if I or my loved one prefer to receive care at home?

    You can access elder care services through Soukhya.Health, an independent elder care provider who offers home-visit services.

  • What are the benefits of receiving Elder care at home compared to inpatient care in a hospital or nursing home?

    Home-based elder care from Soukhya.Health offers comfort, familiarity, personalised attention, reduced risk of infection, and the ability to maintain independence and privacy.
